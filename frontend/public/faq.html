FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions about trials, pricing, report sharing, and how FXCO-Pilot validates trades.

What is FXCO-Pilot? ⌄
FXCO-Pilot is a trade validation tool. It doesn’t predict price — it checks your setup quality: structure, liquidity context, alignment, and risk plan, then gives a disciplined verdict (TAKE / TAKE IF / WAIT / AVOID).
Key idea: decision support, not signals
I buy signals. Why should I use FXCO-Pilot? ⌄
Signals can be right but still be bad executions for your timing, risk, or context. FXCO-Pilot helps you confirm whether the signal has a sensible entry/SL/TP, whether structure supports the bias, and whether the trade is worth taking.
My OTP / trial code is not arriving. What do I do? ⌄
First check Spam/Promotions. If you still don’t see it:
• Ensure the sending domain is verified on your email provider (Resend).
• Confirm your backend has RESEND_API_KEY and RESEND_FROM_EMAIL.
• Try again after 60 seconds (there’s a cooldown).
I paid via Paystack but I’m still locked. Why? ⌄
Most times it’s because the app didn’t verify the transaction reference. Make sure your frontend calls:
GET /api/paystack/verify?reference=XXXX
after payment, and that the backend currency/amount match your Paystack settings.
Tip: confirm PAYSTACK_CURRENCY + amount
My share link says “Report not found (or expired)” ⌄
Share links expire based on REPORT_SHARE_TTL_DAYS. If it’s expired, generate a new report and share again. Also confirm your Supabase table fxco_reports is set up correctly and Supabase env vars are present on the backend.
What’s the difference between “timeframe (style)” and “chart timeframe”? ⌄
Timeframe (style) is how you trade: Scalp / Intraday / Swing.
Chart timeframe is the actual TF you’re looking at (e.g. 15m, 1h, 4h).
Your backend can be set to honor chart_tf as the primary structure driver while keeping timeframe as style.
Does FXCO-Pilot guarantee a win? ⌄
No. It’s not a “win machine.” It’s a discipline machine. It helps you avoid low-quality entries and emotional mistakes, but risk is always real in markets.
What’s the best way to use FXCO-Pilot for real trading? ⌄
1) Paste the signal or your idea with entry/SL/TP.
2) Add how long you plan to hold (e.g. “hold 2 hours”).
3) If you have a chart timeframe, send it as chart_tf (e.g. 15m / 1h).
4) Follow the verdict and conditions: if it says WAIT, you wait.
