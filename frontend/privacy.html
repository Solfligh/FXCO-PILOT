Privacy Policy Minimal Data

Last updated:

1) What We Collect

FXCO-PILOT is designed to operate with minimal data collection. Depending on how the Service is hosted and configured, we may collect:

  • Inputs you provide (signals you paste, and charts you upload) to generate analysis.
  • Basic technical data (e.g., IP address, user-agent, timestamps) for security, abuse prevention, and performance.
  • Optional analytics if enabled by the site owner (e.g., page views and general usage trends).

2) How We Use Data

  • To provide analysis output and improve reliability.
  • To protect the Service from spam, abuse, and attacks.
  • To understand general usage patterns (only if analytics are enabled).

3) Charts & Signal Content

If you upload a chart image or paste a signal, it is processed to generate your requested analysis. Do not upload sensitive personal data.

4) Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only when necessary to operate the Service (e.g., infrastructure providers), or if required by law.

5) Retention

Retention depends on the hosting configuration. Some setups may store logs for security and debugging for a limited period. If “Previous Analyses” is shown in the UI, it is displayed within the browser session unless you have a backend saving it.

6) Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the Service. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7) Your Choices

  • You can avoid uploading charts and use text-only signals.
  • You can stop using the Service at any time.
  • If analytics cookies are used, you may control them through your browser settings.

8) Children

The Service is not intended for children and should not be used by anyone under the age of majority in their jurisdiction.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10) Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via the Solfligh Tech website.
